Instructions

Welcome to the Knowledge Quiz Game!

Your goal is to find a famous person using the search feature and then complete sentences about them using the correct form of the verb "to be."

Here's how to play:

  1. Enter a Name: Type the name of a famous person into the search box and press Enter or click the search button.
  2. View Search Results: You will see a list of results matching your search term. Click on a result to get more details about that person.
  3. Read the Abstract: An abstract from Wikipedia will be displayed. Read the abstract carefully.
  4. Complete the Cloze Text: In the abstract, you will see some sentences with blanks where the verb "to be" is missing. Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb "to be" (e.g., am, is, are, was, were).
  5. Check Your Answers: Once you've filled in the blanks, click the "Check Answers" button to see if you got the answers correct. The game will provide feedback on your answers.

For example, if the abstract says "Albert Einstein ____ a famous physicist," you should fill in "was" to complete the sentence correctly.

Good luck, and enjoy learning about famous people while practicing your verb forms!
