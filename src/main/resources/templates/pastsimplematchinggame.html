Instructions

Welcome to the Verb Practice Game!

Your goal is to match the present tense form of a verb with its correct past tense form. For example, match "play" with "played" or "eat" with "ate".

Here's how to play:

  1. Click on the cards to flip them and reveal the verb forms.
  2. Match pairs of verb forms, where each present tense card must be matched with its corresponding past tense card.
  3. Score points for each correct match. If you flip two cards that do not match, they will flip back over after a short delay.
  4. Complete the game by matching all pairs of cards to win.

To practice different sets of verbs, you can select a difficulty level:

  • Level 1: Basic verbs with regular forms.
  • Level 2: A mix of regular and irregular verbs.
  • Level 3: More challenging verbs with irregular forms.
  • Level 4: Very hard verbs with more complex irregular forms.
  • Level 5: Expert level with less common and complex irregular verbs.

To change the difficulty level, click on the corresponding button for the level you wish to practice. The game will reset with the new difficulty, including resetting your score and attempts.

Good luck and have fun practicing your verbs!
