Instructions

Welcome to the Pronunciation Game!

Objective:

Your goal is to practice and improve your pronunciation of the English alphabet and words.

How to Play:

  • Click on a Letter: The game displays letters from the English alphabet. Click on any letter to hear its pronunciation.
  • Highlight Letters: After selecting a letter, the game will highlight occurrences of that letter in the text provided.
  • Pronounce Words: When you hover over or click on highlighted words, you will hear the pronunciation of the entire word.

Interactive Features:

  • Highlighting: As you click on letters, the game will highlight those letters in the text to help you visualize their occurrence.
  • Pronunciation Feedback: Use the text-to-speech feature to hear how each letter and word is pronounced. This will help you practice and understand the correct pronunciation.

Additional Tips:

  • Practice Regularly: Regular practice will help improve your pronunciation and understanding of the English language.
  • Explore Different Words: Try different words to hear various pronunciations and improve your skills.

Good Luck and Have Fun! Enjoy the game and feel free to repeat exercises to enhance your pronunciation skills.
