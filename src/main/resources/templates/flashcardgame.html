How to Play the Quiz Game: Guess the Translation

Welcome! This game is designed to help you practice language translation by guessing the correct English translation of the given word.

1. View the Image:

Each round, you will see an image that represents the word in the foreign language (in this case, Italian). Use the image to help you guess the correct English translation.

2. Select the Correct Translation:

Below the image, you'll see a set of multiple-choice options. Click on the option that you think is the correct English translation of the Italian word displayed.

3. Get Feedback:

After selecting an option, the game will show you whether your choice was correct or incorrect. Use this feedback to improve your language skills.

4. Proceed to the Next Round:

After viewing the feedback, click the "Next" button to move on to the next image and word combination.

5. Track Your Progress:

Your score will be updated with each round. Try to get as many correct answers as possible to track your learning progress.

6. Image Information:

If available, you can click on the image to get more information about its source, including licensing or additional details.

Good Luck! Have fun guessing the correct translations and improving your language skills.
