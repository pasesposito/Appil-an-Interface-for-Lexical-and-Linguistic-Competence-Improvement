Game of the Goose

Get ready for an exciting board game that tests your knowledge of the verb "to have" and "to do"! The Game of the Goose is a classic game where you move around the board by rolling dice, but with a twist—each space has a question or sentence to complete using the correct form of "to have" or "to do." It’s a great way to practice while having fun.

Here’s how it works:

  • Roll the dice and move your piece the corresponding number of spaces on the board.
  • When you land on a space, you’ll see a sentence with a blank. Your task is to fill in the blank with the correct form of "to have" or "to do."
  • If you answer correctly, you stay on that space. If not, you move back one space.
  • The first player to reach the end of the board wins!

Example: You land on a space with the sentence "They ____ three cats." Your answer should be "have." If correct, you remain on that space and wait for your next turn.

This game is a fun way to reinforce what you’ve learned about "to have" and "to do," as well as to improve your quick thinking and decision-making skills.
