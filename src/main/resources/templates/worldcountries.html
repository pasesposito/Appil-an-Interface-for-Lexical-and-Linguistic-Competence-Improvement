How to Play: Discover Flags and Nationalities

Welcome to the World Map Game!

In this game, you will explore different countries on a world map. Click on a country to learn more about it, including its flag and how to pronounce the nationality. Here's how to get started:

1. Explore the Map:

Use your mouse or touch screen to navigate around the world map. You can zoom in and out and drag the map to explore different regions.

2. Click on a Country:

When you click on a country, a pop-up will appear showing the country's flag and the name of the nationality.

3. Listen to the Pronunciation:

In the pop-up, you'll see an option to listen to how the nationality is pronounced. Click the play button to hear the correct pronunciation.

4. Learn and Explore:

Feel free to click on as many countries as you like to discover different flags and nationalities. This is a great way to familiarize yourself with world geography and improve your language skills.

5. Challenge Yourself:

Try to remember the flags and pronunciations for different countries. Can you identify them without looking next time?

Enjoy the Game!

Have fun exploring the world and learning about different countries and cultures.
